    Determine if a word or phrase is an isogram. An isogram (also known as a "nonpattern word") is a word or phrase without a repeating letter, however spaces and hyphens are allowed to appear multiple times. Examples of isograms:

    • lumberjacks
    • background
    • downstream
    • six-year-old
    The word isograms, however, is not an isogram, because the s repeats
